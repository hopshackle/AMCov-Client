The table shows the number of seasons a magus has been in the covenant (measured from the number of seasons that have an entry on the Lab History page), the number of seasons service they have provided to the covenant, and seasons owed/owing to their sodales. Reading a line horizontally for a magus, a positive number indicates net seasons provided to the magus in the column header, and hence that they are owed, while a negative number indicates a net debt to the magus.
